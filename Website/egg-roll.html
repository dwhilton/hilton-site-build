Aunt Rosie's Egg Rolls

About Aunt Rosie

Aunt Rosie was such a good cook, she didn't use actual measurements. Everything she cooked had an exotice flavor to it.

My mother always asked her about the spices that was used, but she claimed that there was nothing special added.

Ingredients

Note: No measurements here, you'll just have to wing it!

  • 1lb Ground Pork
  • Diced Cabbage...maybe half a small head or you can add more later...
  • Fresh Bean Sprouts
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Package of Egg Roll Wraps
  • Egg(s)

The veggie mix can be as much as you want, but don't over burden your pork so that you can't taste it.

Remember...the bigger your mix the more egg rolls you'll make. Recoomend that you start small the first time

Instructions

  1. Fry and drain the ground pork until it's fully cooked and no longer pink.
  2. Lightly stir fry your veggie mix. Keep it crunchy.
  3. Mix meat into the veggie mix.
  4. Add salt and pepper.
  5. In a bowl, crack and beat (whisk) them eggs.
  6. Lay wrap with a point facing away from you.
  7. Place a small amount of mixture at the top of the wrap.
  8. Roll the wrap towards you until the next two side points.
  9. Take the bowl of whisked egg yolk paint the points of the wrap...think of it like your gluing paper.
  10. Fold them in.
  11. Then roll to final point, and paint with yolk some more to seal.
  12. Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.

If you make a lot some can be frozen for later!
