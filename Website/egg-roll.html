Aunt Rosie's Egg Rolls

About Aunt Rosie

Aunt Rosie was such a good cook, she didn't use actual measurements. Everything she cooked had an exotic flavor to it. My mother always asked her about the spices that were used, but she claimed that there was nothing special added.

Ingredients

Note: No measurements here, you'll just have to wing it!

  • 1lb Ground Pork
  • Diced Cabbage
  • Fresh Bean Sprouts
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Egg Roll Wraps
  • Egg(s)

The veggie mix can be as much as you want...

Instructions

  1. Fry and drain the ground pork...
  2. Lightly stir fry your veggie mix...
  3. Mix meat into the veggie mix...
  4. Add salt and pepper...
  5. Beat the eggs...
  6. Lay wrap with a point facing away...
  7. Place mixture...
  8. Roll the wrap...
  9. Use egg yolk to seal...
  10. Fold sides...
  11. Roll to final point...
  12. Deep fry until golden brown...

If you make a lot, some can be frozen for later!
